the stories

Yum! For Eye and Heart!

What is it?

"YUM" is a collection of short stories and whimsically delightful graphics that are designed to delight the reader and inspire at the same time.

Illustrated short stories

The title of the book is taken from the story "YUM", a commentary about Jelly Beans and the lesson that they can teach us.

Other Stories

"Purple Pants and Go Go Boots", is a short recant of a colorful Aunt, and teaches a lesson about embracing life.
