Yum! For Eye and Heart!

What is it?

"YUM" is a collection of short stories and whimsically delightful graphics that are designed to delight the reader and inspire at the same time.

Illustrated short stories

The title of the book is taken from the story "YUM", a commentary about Jelly Beans and the lesson that they can teach us.

Other Stories

"Purple Pants and Go Go Boots", is a short recant of a colorful Aunt, and teaches a lesson about embracing life.

Excerpts from Yum!

...from "Mame"

At about 2:pm her car pulled alongside our home. The moment she stepped from the vehicle, i was whisked away into that particular brand of delight that can be bestowed only from a really well-executed drama. She absolutely looked for all the world simultaneously madcap and mournful. Her hair swirled in every direction as if styled by an enlightened hurricane. I was riveted to the moment. she had dressed herself in flowing floor length black fabric accented with tons of multi-colored beads that danced circles around her throat in the sunlight like bright painted savages dancing before ritualized flames and long passed ancestors....

...from "Places"

I am aware with increase that my life has entered into newness. This newness has so very little to do with my past, yet all and everything entire to do with my past passed. With every turn of the wheel the train takes me from who i have been, closer to the place that i am to be found. I am riding a train into the places that i must go....
